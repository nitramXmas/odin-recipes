Pizza time !

Description

If you refrain from grain, but can't go without pizza...you've got to try this version with a 1-ingredient crust made just from cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups shredded part-skin mozzarella
  • 5 cherry tomatoes
  • 4 small fresh mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat a 10-inch nin-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Arrange tomatoes slices,basil leaves, and fresh mozzarella slices on the melded cheese
  3. Remove skillet from heat and loosen pizza with a spatula
