WMiam Miam Morue

Description

There are certain things that if I see on a menu, I will almost always order them, and brandade is one of those things

This should always be baked and eaten piping hot, ideally with some homemade crostini.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salted cod fish
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish and set dish on a baking sheet.
  2. Rinse salt cod under cold water, transfer to a sealable container and cover with several inches of cold water.
  3. Stir cod, milk, thyme, bay leaves, and cayenne pepper together in a pot over medium-high heat.
